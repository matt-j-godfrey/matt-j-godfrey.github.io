RED 25 – Rencontres Exobiologiques pour Doctorants

← Back to all events

This astrobiology school covered planetary atmospheres, habitability, and atmospheric composition, providing a broader context for my research and valuable interdisciplinary discussions. As part of the school, I contributed to a group project that designed a conceptual interstellar mission to assess the habitability of an exoplanet.

I was awarded a fee waiver and accommodation support by the organisers, following a successful funding application.
