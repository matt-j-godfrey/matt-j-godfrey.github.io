BOWIE-ALIGN Meeting

← Back to all events

I attended the BOWIE-ALIGN meeting (Bristol, Oxford, Warwick, Imperial, Exeter - A spectral Light Investigation into hot gas Giant origiNs), which focused on developing successful JWST observing proposals and provided insights into the panel review process, including strategies for proposal writing, identifying common pitfalls, and understanding panel expectations. Following the meeting, I plan to lead a JWST proposal for the next cycle and will participate in an upcoming mock JWST review panel designed to give early-career members hands-on experience evaluating proposals.

More information on BOWIE: https://bowiecollaboration.github.io/index.html
