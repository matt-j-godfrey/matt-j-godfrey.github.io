ANTARESS Workshop

← Back to all events

I attended the Advanced and Neat Techniques for the Accurate Retrieval of Exoplanetary and Stellar Spectra (ANTARESS) Workshop at the Geneva Observatory, which focused on standardised methods for processing high-resolution spectroscopy datasets. The workshop covered techniques for extracting stellar and planetary spectra, mitigating instrumental effects, and improving data reproducibility.

These methods align closely with my work, and I plan to incorporate aspects of the workflow (particularly in spectral extraction and noise correction) into my future analyses of exoplanet atmospheres.

More information on the ANTARESS workflow: https://www.astro.unige.ch/~bourriev/antaress/doc/html/index.html
