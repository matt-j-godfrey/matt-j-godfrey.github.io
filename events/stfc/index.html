STFC Introduction to Astronomy Summer School 2024

← Back to all events

This summer school gave me a broad introduction to key astrophysical topics, ranging from planetary systems, to gravitational waves, to helioseismology. It also included some sessions on programming, public engagement, and PhD skills, which were helpful for both my research and professional development. This all especially helped bridge the gap from my Masters studies into the PhD. It was also both useful and interesting to get to know all the other first year PhD students from all over the Uk.

More information on the STFC summer schools can be found here: https://www.ukri.org/what-we-do/developing-people-and-skills/stfc/training/types-of-training/short-courses-and-summer-schools/
