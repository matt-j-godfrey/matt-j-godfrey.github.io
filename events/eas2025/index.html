EAS 2025 – European Astronomical Society Annual Meeting

← Back to all events

I attended the European Astronomical Society Annual Meeting (EAS 2025) online. It provided a useful opportunity to hear about the latest results in exoplanet science. The breadth of sessions helped me situate my work within the wider European context, and several talks on transmission spectroscopy and atmospheric retrieval methods were particularly relevant to my research.

While I didn’t present at this meeting, it was valuable to observe how researchers frame their results and communicate complex findings to an international audience.

More information: https://eas.unige.ch/EAS2025/
